# Support

ShopStack is managed by Brandon at Woodhead Tech. Your monthly fee includes
support for your system.

---

## How to reach support

**Email (preferred):** brandon@woodhead.tech

Include:
- What service is affected (email, file storage, invoicing, online store)
- What you were trying to do
- What error you saw (a screenshot helps a lot)

**Phone:** 425-344-2942 (text or call)

**Book a call:** [cal.com/brandon-woodward-3nlfbd](https://cal.com/brandon-woodward-3nlfbd)

## Response times

| Day | Response target |
|-----|----------------|
| Monday–Friday | Same day (usually within a few hours) |
| Saturday–Sunday | Best-effort, next business day at latest |

If something is completely down (you can't send email at all, or all services are

## What's covered

Your monthly fee covers:

- Keeping all services running (email, file storage, invoicing, online store)
- Fixing issues caused by the system (software crashes, certificate renewals, updates)
- Adding or removing email accounts and aliases
- Password resets for any service
- Answering questions about how to use the system

---

## What's not covered (but available as add-on work)

- Major customization or new features
- Data migration from other systems
- Help with third-party services not part of your ShopStack (e.g., your website, social media)
- Training sessions beyond what's in this documentation

Add-on work is billed at $100/hr. A short quote is provided before any billable
work begins.

---

## Before contacting support

Check the FAQ and relevant docs section first — many common questions are answered there.

If something seems broken:
1. Try refreshing the page or restarting the app
2. Check if the issue is just on one device (try another device or browser)
3. If it affects all devices: email brandon@woodhead.tech right away

---

## Status monitoring

Your system status is available at:

`https://status.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech`

This page shows whether each service is up or down. If everything shows green
but you're still having trouble, the issue may be specific to your device or network.
