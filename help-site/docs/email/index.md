# Business Email

Your ShopStack includes professional email at your own domain, powered by Mailcow.

**Your email addresses** look like: `yourname@yourbusiness.com`

**Your admin panel:** `https://mail.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech`

---

## What you can do

- Send and receive email from any device
- Create mailboxes for each staff member
- Set up shared aliases (info@, appointments@, etc.)
- Access webmail from any browser
- Connect any email client (Outlook, Apple Mail, Thunderbird, Gmail app)

---

## Webmail

If you don't want to set up an email client, you can use webmail in your browser:

1. Go to `https://mail.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech`
2. Log in with your email address and password
3. You'll see your inbox, can compose messages, manage folders, etc.

Webmail works on any device with a browser — phone, tablet, or computer.
