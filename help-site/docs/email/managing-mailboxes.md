# Managing Mailboxes

You can add mailboxes for new staff, create shared email aliases, and reset
passwords from the Mailcow admin panel.

**Admin panel:** `https://mail.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech`

Log in with the admin credentials from Brandon's handoff email.

---

## Add a mailbox for a new staff member

1. Log in to the admin panel
2. Click **Email** in the top menu → **Mailboxes**
3. Click **Add mailbox**
4. Fill in:
   - **Username** — the part before the @ (e.g., `sarah`)
   - **Full name** — the staff member's name (shows in email "From" field)
   - **Password** — create a strong password and share it securely
   - **Quota** — leave at the default (or increase for heavy users)
5. Click **Add**

The new mailbox is ready immediately. Share the login URL and credentials with
the staff member so they can set up their email client.

---

## Create a shared email alias

Aliases let multiple people receive email at a single address (e.g., info@, appointments@).
Email sent to the alias is delivered to whoever you choose.

1. Log in to the admin panel
2. Click **Email** → **Aliases**
3. Click **Add alias**
4. Enter the alias address (e.g., `info@yourdomain.com`)
5. In **Goto** field, enter the real mailbox(es) that should receive the email
   - Separate multiple addresses with commas
6. Click **Add**

Email to `info@yourdomain.com` will now be delivered to the mailboxes you specified.

---

## Reset a staff member's password

1. Log in to the admin panel
2. Click **Email** → **Mailboxes**
3. Find the mailbox and click **Edit** (pencil icon)
4. Enter and confirm the new password
5. Click **Save**

Let the staff member know their new password. They'll need to update it in their
email client settings too.

---

## Change your own password

You can change your mailbox password without going through the admin panel:

1. Go to `https://mail.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech`
2. Log in with your email and current password
3. Click your name in the top right → **Settings**
4. Click **Password** → enter your current and new passwords
5. Click **Save**

---

## Remove a mailbox (staff member left)

:::caution
Deleting a mailbox permanently deletes all email in it. Export or forward any
important messages first.
:::

1. Log in to the admin panel
2. Click **Email** → **Mailboxes**
3. Check the box next to the mailbox
4. Click **Delete selected** → confirm

Consider creating an alias pointing to another mailbox first, so any emails still
sent to the old address don't bounce.
