# Invoicing

Your ShopStack includes Invoice Ninja — professional invoicing software that lets
you create and send invoices with Stripe payment links.

**Your invoicing panel:** `https://billing.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech`

---

## What you can do

- Create and send professional invoices by email
- Accept online payment via Stripe (credit card, debit card, ACH)
- Set up recurring invoices for monthly clients
- Track which invoices are paid, overdue, or pending
- Add your business logo and customize invoice appearance
- Create quotes that convert to invoices when approved

---

## Your first time logging in

The first time you visit `https://billing.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech`, you'll be
taken through a brief setup wizard:

1. **Company details** — enter your business name, address, phone number
2. **Logo** — upload your business logo (PNG or SVG recommended)
3. **Currency** — select USD (or your local currency)
4. **Payment gateway** — connect Stripe if you have an account (see [Stripe payments](/invoicing/stripe-payments))

You can skip the Stripe step and add it later.
