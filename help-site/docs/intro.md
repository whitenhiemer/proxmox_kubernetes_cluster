# Welcome to ShopStack

ShopStack is your complete business system — email, file storage, and invoicing —
all in one place, managed for you by Woodhead Tech.

## What's included in your ShopStack

| Service | What it does | Your URL |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Business Email** | Professional email at your domain | mail.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech |
| **File Storage** | Shared files and folders for your team | files.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech |
| **Invoicing** | Create and send invoices with Stripe payment links | billing.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech |
| **Status Page** | Live uptime monitoring | status.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech |

ShopStack Online clients also get:
| Service | What it does | Your URL |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Online Store** | WooCommerce storefront | shop.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech |

## First steps after setup

1. **Set up your email client** — add your business email to Outlook, Gmail, Apple Mail, or your phone. [Email client setup →](/email/email-client-setup)

2. **Install Nextcloud on your computer** — sync your business files automatically. [Desktop sync setup →](/files/desktop-sync)

3. **Create your first invoice** — send a professional invoice with a Stripe payment link. [Create an invoice →](/invoicing/create-invoice)

4. **Connect Stripe to invoicing** — accept online payments directly from invoices. [Set up Stripe payments →](/invoicing/stripe-payments)
