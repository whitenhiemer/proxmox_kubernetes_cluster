# Managing Products

Add and manage the products in your online store from the WordPress admin panel.

**Admin panel:** `https://shop.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech/wp-admin`

---

## Add a new product

1. Log in to the admin panel
2. In the left sidebar, click **Products** → **Add New**
3. Fill in the product details:

**Product name** — what customers see as the title.

**Description** — appears below the product name on the product page.
Use the editor to format text, add bullet points, etc.

**Short description** — a brief summary shown near the price.

**Product data** (in the lower section):
- **Regular price** — the normal selling price
- **Sale price** (optional) — discounted price; you can set start/end dates

**Product image** — click **Set product image** (right sidebar) to upload a photo.
Use clear, well-lit photos. JPG or PNG, at least 800x800px.

**Product gallery** — add additional photos.

**Categories** — organize products into groups (e.g., "Shirts", "Accessories").
Create new categories in the right sidebar if needed.

4. Click **Publish** when ready, or **Save Draft** to come back to it later.

---

## Edit an existing product

1. Click **Products** in the left sidebar
2. Hover over the product name → click **Edit**
3. Make your changes → click **Update**

---

## Remove a product from the store

To temporarily hide a product without deleting it:
1. Edit the product
2. Change **Status** from **Published** to **Draft** (top right)
3. Click **Update** — the product disappears from the store but stays in your admin

To permanently delete:
1. In the Products list, hover over the product → click **Trash**

---

## Digital products (downloadable files)

For digital products (PDFs, patterns, templates, etc.):

1. Create the product as above
2. In the **Product data** section, check **Virtual** and **Downloadable**
3. Under **Downloadable files**, click **Add file** → upload your file
4. Set a **Download limit** (how many times per purchase) if desired
5. Publish the product

After payment, customers receive a download link automatically by email.
