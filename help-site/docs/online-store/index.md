# Online Store

:::info ShopStack Online clients only
This section applies to clients on the ShopStack Online plan. If you're on
ShopStack (brick-and-mortar), your system doesn't include an online store.
:::

Your ShopStack Online includes a WooCommerce storefront powered by WordPress.

**Your store:** `https://shop.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech`
**Admin panel:** `https://shop.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech/wp-admin`

---

## What's included

- A full WooCommerce online store
- Product listings with photos, descriptions, and pricing
- Stripe checkout for credit card and debit card payments
- Automatic order notifications by email
- Order management dashboard

---

## Logging in to your store admin

1. Go to `https://shop.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech/wp-admin`
2. Log in with the WordPress admin username and password from your handoff email

The WordPress dashboard is your control center for the store.
