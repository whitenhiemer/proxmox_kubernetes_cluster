# Mobile App

The Nextcloud mobile app lets you access and upload files from your phone or tablet.

**Download:**
- iPhone/iPad: search "Nextcloud" in the App Store (free, by Nextcloud GmbH)
- Android: search "Nextcloud" in Google Play (free, by Nextcloud GmbH)

---

## Connect to your ShopStack

1. Open the Nextcloud app
2. Tap **Log in**
3. Enter your server URL: `https://files.YOUR_DOMAIN.woodhead.tech`
4. Tap **Connect**
5. Log in with your Nextcloud username and password
6. Tap **Grant access**

Your files will appear in the app.

---

## Browsing and uploading files

**Browse files:** Tap folders to navigate, tap files to preview them.

**Upload a photo or file:**
1. Tap **+** (bottom of screen)
2. Choose **Upload files** or **Upload photos**
3. Select the file from your phone

**Download a file to your phone:**
- Tap the file → tap the download icon (or three dots → **Download**)

---

## Auto-upload photos (optional)

You can set the app to automatically upload photos you take to Nextcloud:

1. Tap the menu (≡) → **Settings**
2. Tap **Auto upload**
3. Toggle on **Photo upload**
4. Choose the folder in Nextcloud where photos should go

This is useful if you take photos at your business (products, work orders, etc.)
and want them backed up and accessible to your team.

---

## Offline access

To make a file or folder available offline (viewable without internet):

- Tap the three dots (…) next to the file or folder → **Make available offline**

Offline files take up space on your phone but can be viewed and edited without a connection.
